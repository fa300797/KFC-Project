Established in 2014, KFC’s CSR platform “Mitao Bhook” transcends hunger to encompass hunger for education, a better life and bigger aspirations under its 3 pillars of Education, Diversity and Inclusion. This is integral to KFC Pakistan’s vision of becoming a 365–day giving company and is fuelled by the strong commitment to being a contributing member of our community.

KFC Higher Education Scholarship Program

In collaboration with Orange Tree Foundation, Mitao Bhook has launched the KFC Higher Education Scholarship Program to encourage talented students unable to continue their educational journey due to financial constraints.

Our Commitments

We have adopted 4 TCF Schools and are educating more than 800 students in Gadaap West, Faisalabad, Quetta, and Muzaffargarh.

We are supporting the DEWA Trust in operating their Institute of Special and Inclusive Education.

We are proudly running the Deaf Reach KFC Campus and providing education and training to 200 deaf students in Karachi.

In partnership with CYTE Foundation, Mitao Bhook runs TEGS – KFC Campus in Lahore, providing quality education to 200+ students.
